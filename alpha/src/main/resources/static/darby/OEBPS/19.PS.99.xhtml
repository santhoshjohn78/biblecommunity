Psalms 99

1 Jehovah reigneth: let the peoples tremble. He sitteth between the cherubim: let the earth be moved. 2 Jehovah is great in Zion, and he is high above all the peoples. 3 They shall praise thy great and terrible name, -- it is holy! -- 4 And the strength of the king that loveth justice. Thou hast established equity: it is thou that executest judgment and righteousness in Jacob. 5 Exalt Jehovah our God* and worship at his footstool. He is holy!

6 Moses and Aaron among his priests, and Samuel among them that call upon his name: they called unto Jehovah, and he answered them. 7 He spoke unto them in the pillar of cloud: they kept his testimonies, and the statute that he gave them. 8 Jehovah, our God* thou answeredst them: a forgiving Â·God* wast thou unto them, though thou tookest vengeance of their doings. 9 Exalt Jehovah our God* and worship at the hill of his holiness; for holy is Jehovah our God* .
