Psalms 132

1 A Canticle in steps: of David. Behold, how good and how pleasing it is for brothers to dwell in unity.

2 It is like the ointment on the head that descended to the beard, the beard of Aaron, which descended to the hem of his garment.

3 It is like the dew of Hermon, which descended from mount Zion. For in that place, the Lord has commanded a blessing, and life, even unto eternity.
